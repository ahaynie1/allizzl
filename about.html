About

I love improving systems and creating work that is impactful, engaging, and fun.

I believe in leading with kindness and using visuals as a tool to promote equity, beauty, and enjoyable experiences.

I received my Bachelor of Design in Visual Communication Design and my certificate in User Experience Design in June 2023. Throughout my last two years of college, I worked for the office of Student Affairs as content team lead, web designer, and graphic designer.

Outside of work you'll find me on the pickleball court, thrifting, or tending to my many plants.
