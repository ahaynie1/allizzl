About

I'm passionate about improving systems and creating work that is impactful, engaging, and fun. I believe in leading with kindness and using visuals as a tool to promote equity, beauty, and enjoyable experiences.

I received my Bachelor of Design in Visual Communication Design and my certificate in User Experience Design in June 2023. During my last two years of college, I worked for the office of Student Affairs as content team lead, web designer, and graphic designer.
