EWU Student Affairs Website

Eastern Washington University
Adobe Suite

Project Overview

I was tasked with completing a UX audit of the Student Affairs Website and releasing an updated version of the site with a new visual identity and navigational hierarchy.

Before: Key problems

  • Lack of visual hierarchy and scannability
  • Inconsistent, illogical navigational/organizational structure
  • Visually unappealing and outdated

Research

We completed a competitive analysis of similar sites at other universities. I also did a visual audit of more modern internal and external sites within EWU to pull together a cohesive visual identity.

Process

1) Hierarchical organization and card sorts

2) Sketches & Wireframes

3) Development and Feedback I built the site in wordpress, guided by input and feedback from the university president, the Dean of Students, incoming and outgoing Vice Presidents of Student Affairs, and other Student Affairs admin.

4) Finished Product
