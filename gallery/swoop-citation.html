School Spirit Citation

Eastern Washington University
Illustrator, InDesign, Procreate

What began as a joke among EWU administration evolved into a tongue in cheek advertisement for the campus store. Students came to campus wearing fan gear from a different university. The idea that was brought to me was for a list-type citation, playfully condemning their lack of school spirit while also serving as a coupon to buy merch from the correct school. I decided to incorporate our beloved mascot, Swoop, and lean into the humor.
